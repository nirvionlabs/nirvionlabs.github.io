Privacy Policy

How CSQuiz handles your data — clearly and honestly.

CSQuiz — by Nirvion Labs
Your privacy is important to us. CSQuiz is designed to work without collecting any personal information. This policy explains exactly what data is involved when you use the app.
1
📶

Internet Connection Requirement

CSQuiz requires an internet connection to download MCQ subjects, check for updates, and load certain services such as advertisements.

If the device remains offline for an extended period of time, the app may request an internet connection before continuing to ensure proper functionality.

2
🔍

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require account creation, email address, name, or phone number.

On first install, the app generates an anonymous random identifier (UUID) stored on your device. This ID contains no personal information and cannot be used to identify you. It is used solely for anonymous analytics and crash reporting.

Your study progress (answered questions, test scores, bookmarks, and daily streak) is stored exclusively on your device using encrypted local storage. This data never leaves your device.
3
📢

Advertising

CSQuiz uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use your device's Advertising ID, cookies, and other device information to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

This data collection is handled by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Advertisements are not shown during the first 48 hours after installation. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings at any time.
4
🔗

Third-Party Services

The application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Google Play Services
  • Google Firebase Analytics (Usage Analytics)
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics (Crash Reporting)

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

5
📥

Content Downloads

When you download MCQ subjects, the app sends a request to our secure server containing only the subject ID. MCQ content is returned and stored on your device encrypted using RSA/AES-256 encryption.

No personal data, device identifiers, or location data is sent to our server during subject downloads.
6
👤

No Account Required

CSQuiz does not require user registration or login. There are no user accounts, usernames, passwords, or profiles associated with this application. The app works directly without any sign-in process.

7
💾

Local Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Downloaded MCQ subject content (encrypted)
  • Your attempt history and answer records
  • Test scores and results
  • Bookmarked questions
  • Daily study streak data
  • App theme preference
  • Anonymous device identifier (UUID)
All of this data belongs entirely to you and remains on your device.
8
🗑️

Data Deletion

You can clear all your progress data (attempts, bookmarks, and streak) at any time from Settings → Data → Clear All Progress.

To remove all data including downloaded subject content, simply uninstall the application from your device. All locally stored data will be permanently removed.

9
👶

Children's Privacy

The app is designed for students studying computer science subjects and is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the app, no personal data has been collected as the app does not collect personal information from any user.

10
🔄

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected within the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
