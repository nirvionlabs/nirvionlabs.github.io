Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

CSQuiz is developed by Nirvion Labs. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

1. Internet Connection Requirement

CSQuiz requires an internet connection to download MCQ subjects, check for updates, and load certain services such as advertisements. If the device remains offline for an extended period, the app may request an internet connection to ensure proper functionality.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require account creation, email address, name, or phone number.

On first install, the app generates an anonymous random identifier (UUID) stored on your device. This ID contains no personal information and cannot be used to identify you. It is used solely for anonymous analytics and crash reporting.

Your study progress (answered questions, test scores, bookmarks, and daily streak) is stored exclusively on your device using encrypted local storage. This data never leaves your device.

3. Advertising

CSQuiz uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use your device's Advertising ID, cookies, and other device information to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This data collection is handled by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

4. Third-Party Services

The application uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Google Play Services
  • Google Firebase Analytics (Usage Analytics)
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics (Crash Reporting)

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

5. Content Downloads

When you download MCQ subjects, the app sends a request to our secure server containing only the subject ID. MCQ content is returned and stored on your device encrypted using RSA/AES-256 encryption. No personal data, device identifiers, or location data is sent to our server during subject downloads.

6. No Account Required

CSQuiz does not require user registration or login. There are no user accounts, usernames, passwords, or profiles associated with this application.

7. Local Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Downloaded MCQ subject content (encrypted)
  • Your attempt history and answer records
  • Test scores and results
  • Bookmarked questions
  • Daily study streak data
  • App theme preference
  • Anonymous device identifier (UUID)

All of this data belongs entirely to you and remains on your device.

8. Data Deletion

You can clear all your progress data at any time from Settings → Data → Clear All Progress. To remove all data including downloaded content, simply uninstall the application.

9. Children's Privacy

The app is designed for students studying computer science subjects and is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected within the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact
