How We Think

Principles we refuse
to compromise

Not marketing words. Constraints we actually build against every day.

🚫

No Accounts, No Drama

Install and use immediately. No sign-up forms, no verification emails, no forgotten passwords. We don't want your identity.

🛡️

Privacy by Default

Your data is yours. We document what we collect, why, and how — in plain English. If we don't need it, we don't touch it.

🎯

One Job, Done Well

We resist feature creep. Each app does exactly what it promises — nothing more, no discovery tabs, no "you might also like".

✨

Craft Over Speed

We'd rather take another week to get it right than ship something mediocre on schedule. Good software is worth the wait.

💬

Honest Communication

Our changelogs say what actually changed. Our policies say what we actually do. No spin, no corporate speak.

🚀

Ship When Ready

Not when a deadline says so. We release when we'd personally recommend it to a friend without a second thought.
