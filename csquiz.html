Download Subjects Offline

Browse and download MCQ subject packs from our server. Once downloaded, practice anytime with no internet required. Content is encrypted and stored securely on your device.

MCQ Practice Mode

Navigate through subjects → categories → topics. Answer MCQs one by one, get instant correct/wrong feedback, and read full explanations after each question. Track completion per topic.

Timed Test Mode

Create custom tests from single or multiple subjects. Choose question count (10–200), set an optional timer (5–120 min), and get a full results breakdown with correct/wrong/skipped counts.

Tap the bookmark icon on any question to save it. All bookmarked questions are grouped by subject and category for easy focused review. Bookmarks persist across app restarts.

Daily Study Streak

Build a consistent study habit. Your streak grows every day you practice and resets if you miss a day. A 7-day activity chart and bar graph visualise your recent progress.

Performance Dashboard

The Home screen shows total subjects downloaded, total MCQs attempted, tests taken, average score, and your top-performing subjects with accuracy percentages.

Auto Subject Updates

Full support for light mode, dark mode, and automatic system theme. Switch any time from Settings with an instant preview.

Study Reminders

Set a daily notification reminder to keep your streak going. Notifications fire at your chosen time even when the app is fully closed.

Privacy First
Your data stays on your device
CSQuiz is designed from the ground up to respect your privacy.
🔒

No personal data collected. Ever.

CSQuiz requires no account, no email, no name, and no phone number. All your progress — attempted questions, test scores, bookmarks, and daily streak — is stored exclusively on your device using encrypted local storage and never transmitted anywhere.

An anonymous random UUID is generated on first install for crash reporting only. It cannot identify you personally in any way. The app uses Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Crashlytics — all disclosed in our Privacy Policy.
